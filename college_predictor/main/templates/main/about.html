OVERVIEW OF OUR PROJECT :

  • As soon as a student completes his/her intermediate education, the first goal of any student is to get into an appropriate Undergraduate College. Students face difficulty inorder to choose a right college. So, our COLLEGE PREDICTOR project helps them in choosing a better college for him/her based on their preferences.

  • College Predictor is a web-based application, where student inputs his/her competitive rank like that of Eamcet, Caste and Gender. These three are the key attributes for any placement in any college under any Board Of Education, especially in India. Our College Predictor only works for Engineering Category.Along with the key fields, user may choose to give their own preferences in various facilities and services offered by the colleges like College Type, Bus Facility, Hostel, Sports, Medical Care, etc.Based on these preferences taken from the student, we shall provide you a match percentile for every college that qualifies you based on your rank. College Predictor displays the list of colleges on a webpage as a response. So, all you need to do is to fill a small form in the Homepage and click on submit. In a few seconds your result shall be displayed. The result(list of colleges) include columns such as Eamcet Code, College Name, Course Code, Place, Contact No, Website, NBA Accreditation, NAAC Rating, NIRF Ranking, Preferences Match %.

  • We have also provided an automated e-mail generator mechanism especially for the authorised users only, which sends the last result queried by the user. So, you need to make sure you need to signed up and logged into your account.

STEPS TO VIEW YOUR RESULT :

  1. Go to Login/SignUp page and register using signup form.
  2. If you are already an existing user, don't forget to login using the Login/SignUp page.
  3. Go to Homepage choose the type of form and fill it with all your preferences and the necessary fields, and submit it.
  4. After a few seconds, you will be redirected to your result
  5. You don't need to note down your result, feel free to use the mail service provided by us.
